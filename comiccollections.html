Where did it start

Star Wars comics have been produced by various comic book publishers since the debut of the 1977 film Star Wars. Marvel Comics launched its original series in 1977, the beginning with a six-issue comic adaptation of the film running for 107 issues, including an adaptation of the Empire Strikes Back. There are 21 books in totaling in 678 issues. 68 mini-series totalling 320 issues. 78 Dark horse legends, one-shot issues. 4 Marvel legends one-shot Issues.

The Creator Of Star Wars

George Lucas, a Canadian artist, writer, and publisher, created the comic books for the film. He started the Marvel Comics universe in 1977 with the publication of his six-issue comic adaptation of the film. He later expanded the Marvel universe to include DC Comics in 1984. George Lucas also created the DC Universe in 1986, which included the publication of his seven-issue comic adaptation of the film. He continued to create comic books for the Star Wars franchise through 2021. George Lucas soon planned a new space fantasy.
